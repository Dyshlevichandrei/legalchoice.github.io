    How do we work?

    Stages

    We get information about your company, agree on the terms of cooperation and enter into a contract

    Determine the areas of accounting, provided the scripts to work, the state, and determine the best system of taxation

    We appoint a personal accountant, chief accountant and auditor. We assign responsibilities and deadlines. You provide the primary documents, and the accountant processes them in the accounting database

    The chief accountant checks the work of the personal accountant on a monthly basis, generates quarterly financial results, calculates taxes, and generates tax returns and reports to government agencies

    The personal accountant makes an inventory of primary accounting documents, makes registers of primary accounting documents and sends them to you

    Once a year we conduct an internal audit of your company's accounting and tax reports
